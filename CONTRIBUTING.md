# 🤝 Contributing to SATARK PRIME

Thank you for your interest in SATARK PRIME — a state-mandated, open-source climate defense grid fighting the stubble burning crisis across the Indo-Gangetic Plain.

We are a small, field-first team. Every contributor here works on a real problem, with real farmers, in real mud. If that's what you're looking for, read on.

**SATARK is open to contributors nationally and internationally.** Field tracks are ground-deployed in West Bengal. Remote tracks are open to anyone, anywhere.

## 🧾 The Deployment Contract

This is not a typical open-source volunteering call. When you join SATARK, you join as an **Operator** — on a formal **3-to-4 month flexible operational trial**, under the official mandate of the Kolaghat BDO and Egra ADA, Government of West Bengal.

| Term | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Duration** | 3–4 months, flexible — your studies or work come first |
| **Exit policy** | Step away at any time, **zero penalty** |
| **On trial completion** | State-Level **Proof of Work certificate** issued |
| **On becoming official Operator** | **Dollar-pegged stipend** for every verified unit of work submitted |
| **Data integrity** | Fraudulent or falsified field reports → immediate removal, no certificate |

> *"You are not a volunteer. You are an Operator."*
> — SATARK Phase-2 Deployment Matrix, DOC-REF: SATARK-DM-P2

## 📬 How to Apply

Send an email to **[reezaalarafat@gmail.com](mailto:reezaalarafat@gmail.com)** with the subject line:

```
[SATARK] Application — <Role> — <Your Name>
```

For example: `[SATARK] Application — AI/ML Engineer — Priya Sharma`

**Your email should include:**

1. **Name & Location** — where you're based (village / city / country)
2. **Role you're applying for** — from the tracks above
3. **Brief background & skills** — what you've done that's relevant; keep it honest, not polished
4. **Why you want to join** — what drew you to this problem specifically
5. **Links** — GitHub, LinkedIn, portfolio, published work, or anything relevant *(optional but helpful)*

No formal CV required. We read every email.

## 💬 Not A Test — But It Tells Us Everything

Before or after applying, we'd love to understand how you think.

👉 **[satark-not-a-test.netlify.app](https://satark-not-a-test.netlify.app)**

This is **not a test** — there are no right or wrong answers, no scoring, no elimination. It's a short exercise designed to give us a clearer picture of how you see the problem, and to give *you* a clearer picture of what SATARK actually is. It takes about 3 minutes and directly informs how we work with you. We strongly recommend doing it before you apply.

## 🧭 What Happens After You Apply

1. **We read it** — usually within a few days
2. **We reply** — either with follow-up questions or a short call / chat
3. **You get onboarded** — given access to the relevant sub-repo, introduced to the team, and assigned a first task

There is no long interview process. If your application is genuine and your skills fit a current need, we move fast.

## 🔧 For Code Contributors (GitHub)

If you'd prefer to contribute code directly before reaching out:

1. **Fork** the repository
2. **Create a branch**: `git checkout -b feature/your-feature-name`
3. **Commit your changes**: `git commit -m "feat: describe what you did"`
4. **Push and open a Pull Request** against `main`

Please keep PRs focused — one feature or fix per PR. Include a short description of what the change does and why.

For bugs or feature requests, open a **GitHub Issue** first so we can discuss before you build.
